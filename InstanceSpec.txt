Instance Spec, v3

* http://www.curse.com/addons/wow/instance-spec

Are you tired of forgetting to change spec to your assigned role before entering? This addon will add a button below dungeon ready dialog that will allow you to quickly switch to your other spec if you need it to match your assigned role.
